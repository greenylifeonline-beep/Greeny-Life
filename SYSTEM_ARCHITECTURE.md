# GREENY LIFE Digital Operating System (GL-DOS)

Version: 1.0

---

# Executive Vision

GREENY LIFE Digital Operating System (GL-DOS) is the central operating platform for the GREENY LIFE brand.

The system manages products, packaging, branding, digital assets, catalogs, export documentation, quotations, and customer-facing content from a single source of truth.

Its purpose is to eliminate duplicated information, standardize operations, and support future growth across global markets.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Single Source of Truth (SSOT)

Every piece of information exists only once.

Examples:

• Product specifications
• Packaging specifications
• Brand colors
• Images
• Certificates

All other systems read from these master records.

---

## 2. Enterprise Scalability

The system must support:

• 10 Products
• 100 Products
• 1000 Products

without changing the architecture.

---

## 3. Modular Design

Every component works independently.

## 4. Consistent Identity

The visual identity never changes.

Only product-specific information changes.
